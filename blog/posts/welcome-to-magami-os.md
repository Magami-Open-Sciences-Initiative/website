At Magami OS, we believe that the future of drug discovery should be shaped by those who understand Africa's health challenges best — African researchers themselves.

## Why Magami OS exists

Africa bears the largest burden of infectious and Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs), yet these diseases receive the least global research funding. This imbalance is not just unfair — it is unscientific. The regions with the greatest need should have access to the most innovative tools and approaches.

```python
# A simple example: our disease targeting approach
diseases = [
    "Malaria", "Tuberculosis", "HIV/AIDS",
    "Schistosomiasis", "Onchocerciasis"
]

for disease in diseases:
    print(f"Targeting: {disease}")
    print(f"Status: Active research collaboration")
```

## Our three pillars

### 1. AI Collaboration
We partner with African researchers at any stage of drug discovery, providing AI expertise and computational resources that might otherwise be inaccessible.

### 2. Training & Capacity Building
Knowledge transfer is at the heart of our mission. We offer comprehensive training programmes for biomedical researchers across Africa.

### 3. Open Source Software
Every tool we develop is released as open source, ensuring that anyone — anywhere — can build upon our work.
