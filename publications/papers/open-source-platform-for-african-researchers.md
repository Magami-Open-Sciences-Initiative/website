The Magami OS platform is an integrated open-source software ecosystem designed to lower the barrier to AI-assisted drug discovery for researchers across Africa. The platform comprises three core modules: (i) a molecular library management system pre-loaded with compound databases relevant to African diseases, (ii) a suite of machine learning models for property prediction and lead optimization, and (iii) a collaborative workspace for distributed research teams.

We benchmarked the platform against commercial alternatives and demonstrate comparable performance across standard drug discovery workflows, while requiring significantly less computational infrastructure.
