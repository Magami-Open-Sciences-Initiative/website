We present a framework for AI-accelerated drug discovery targeting neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) prevalent in Sub-Saharan Africa. Using a combination of deep learning models for molecular property prediction and virtual screening, we identified seven novel compounds with activity against Schistosoma mansoni and Onchocerca volvulus. Three candidates showed favourable pharmacokinetic profiles and low predicted toxicity in preliminary in silico assays.

Our approach demonstrates that AI-driven methods can significantly reduce the time and cost of early-stage drug discovery for diseases that have historically received inadequate research funding.
