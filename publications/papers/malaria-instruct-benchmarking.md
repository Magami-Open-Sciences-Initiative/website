We present a systematic evaluation of five open-source LLMs (Gemma-2 2B, Gemma-2 9B,
TxGemma-2B, TxGemma-9B, and LlaSMol-Mistral-7B) on Malaria-Instruct, a curated instruction-following dataset derived from the ChEMBL Legacy Malaria corpus for Malaria virtual screening, on a rigorous out-of-distribution data split, benchmarked against classical ML models (Random Forest, XGBoost) and frontier proprietary models (Gemini 2.5, OpenAI o3) under few-shot conditions.
